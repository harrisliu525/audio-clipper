FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our breath removal tool.

How does the breath detection work?
Our algorithm analyzes audio waveforms to identify periods of low amplitude that match typical breath patterns. It uses RMS (Root Mean Square) energy calculation to detect these patterns accurately.
What audio formats are supported?
Currently, we support MP3 and WAV formats. We're working on adding more formats in the future.
Is my audio secure when I use this tool?
Yes, all processing is done in your browser. Your audio files are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.
What should I adjust if the detection isn't working well?
Try adjusting the sensitivity slider. Higher values make the detection more sensitive (detecting more breaths), while lower values make it less sensitive. You can also adjust the minimum duration to filter out shorter sounds.
Will this remove all background noise?
No, this tool is specifically designed to target breath sounds. For general noise reduction, we recommend using a dedicated noise reduction tool first, then using AudioClipper for breath removal.
Is there a limit to the audio file size I can process?
In the free version, you can process files up to 100MB in size and up to 10 minutes in length. Paid plans have higher or no limits.

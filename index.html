Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How does BreathCut detect breathing sounds?

BreathCut uses advanced audio analysis algorithms to detect breathing sounds based on their unique acoustic signatures. Our technology analyzes the audio waveform's energy levels, frequency patterns, and duration to identify breathing sounds with high accuracy. You can adjust the sensitivity to fine-tune detection for your specific recording environment.

Will removing breaths make my audio sound unnatural?

Not at all! BreathCut is designed to remove breathing sounds while maintaining natural speech rhythm and flow. Rather than creating abrupt cuts, the tool intelligently adjusts the timing to ensure your audio sounds professional and natural. You can also control the pause duration to match your preference for pacing.

Is there a limit to the file size I can process?

We recommend keeping files under 100MB for optimal browser performance, as all processing happens locally on your device. For longer recordings, you may want to split your audio into smaller segments before processing.

Are my audio files uploaded to your servers?

No. BreathCut processes your audio entirely within your browser. Your files never leave your computer or get uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security for your content.

What audio formats are supported?

BreathCut supports most common audio formats including MP3, WAV, OGG, AAC, FLAC, and M4A. Processed files are downloaded in high-quality WAV format to preserve audio fidelity.

Is BreathCut really completely free?

Yes! BreathCut is 100% free to use with no hidden fees, account creation, or premium features. We believe in making professional audio tools accessible to everyone. The tool is supported by minimal, non-intrusive advertising.
